# USER GUIDE - SHABAKA ADSCREEN

> **INTERNAL USE:** This guide is intended for platform administrators and partner organization managers.

---

## 1. Overview

Shabaka AdScreen is a centralized digital signage management platform. It allows:
*   **Screen Owners (Organizations):** To monetize their audience.
*   **Advertisers:** To broadcast targeted campaigns in a few clicks.

---

## 2. For Organizations (Screen Owners)

### 2.1 Dashboard
Upon login, you access key indicators:
*   **Total Revenue:** Cumulative sold campaigns.
*   **Active Screens:** Number of screens connected in real-time.
*   **Occupancy Rate:** Percentage of sold slots vs available.

### 2.2 Adding a Screen
1.  Go to **Screen Management > Add**.
2.  Define the name, orientation (Landscape/Portrait) and address.
3.  The system generates a **Pairing Code**.
4.  On the physical screen, open the Player URL and enter this code.

### 2.3 Setting Your Prices
To sell advertising, you must configure your grids:
*   **Time Slots:** Define the base price for 15s, 30s, etc. (e.g., 15s = €2).
*   **Time Periods:** Apply multipliers based on the hour (e.g., 18h-20h = x1.5).

### 2.4 Moderation
When an advertiser buys a slot, you receive a notification.
*   Go to **Validations**.
*   Check the content (Quality, Legality).
*   Click **Approve** or **Reject** (with reason).

---

## 3. For Advertisers

### 3.1 Creating a Campaign
1.  Click **Book a Campaign**.
2.  Select one or more screens on the map or list.
3.  Configure the duration (e.g., 15 seconds) and the broadcast period.
4.  The price is calculated automatically.

### 3.2 Technical Specifications
*   **Videos:** MP4 only. Max 100MB. (Videos are automatically converted for optimal playback without interruption).
*   **Images:** High-resolution JPG or PNG (1920x1080 recommended).
*   **Format:** Respect the target screen orientation (Landscape vs Portrait).

### 3.3 Payment and Billing
*   Once the campaign is configured, a **Pending** invoice is generated.
*   Make the bank transfer according to the coordinates provided.
*   Upload the **Payment Proof** in the **My Invoices** tab.
*   Once validated by accounting, your campaign is scheduled.

### 3.4 Tracking Stats
Access **My Stats** to see:
*   The number of actual plays (Impressions).
*   The total display duration per day.
