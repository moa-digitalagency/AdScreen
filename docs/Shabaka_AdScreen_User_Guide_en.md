# Shabaka AdScreen - User Guide

This guide is intended for venue managers (bars, restaurants, malls) and advertisers wishing to broadcast content.

### 1. For Screen Owners (Organizations)

You manage a venue open to the public and wish to monetize your screens.

#### 1.1 Accessing Your Space
Log in at `/login`. You will arrive at your **Dashboard** which displays:
*   The number of active screens.
*   Revenue generated for the current week.
*   Content pending validation.

#### 1.2 Adding a New Screen
1.  Go to **Screens** > **Add**.
2.  Give it a name (e.g., "Main Entrance").
3.  Define the **exact resolution** (e.g., 1920x1080).
4.  Choose the **orientation** (Landscape or Portrait).
5.  Set the **Base Price per Minute** (e.g., €1.00). This rate will serve as the basis for slot calculations.

#### 1.3 Configuring Prices
Once the screen is created, configure the sales options:
*   **Time Slots**: Define sellable durations (10s, 15s, 30s).
*   **Time Periods**: Apply surcharges based on the time of day.
    *   *Example: Create a "Happy Hour" period from 6 PM to 9 PM with a multiplier of x1.5.*

#### 1.4 Managing the Playlist
*   **Validation**: When a client books a slot, you receive a notification. Go to **Content** > **To Validate**. Check that the visual respects your ethical charter.
*   **Self-Promotion**: You can broadcast your own ads (Menu, Events) for free via the **Internal Content** tab. These contents have a slightly lower priority than paid ads.
*   **Overlays**: Add scrolling messages (tickers) at the bottom of the screen for quick announcements.

#### 1.5 Billing
Every Sunday evening, an invoice is generated calculating the commission due to the platform.
1.  Go to **Billing**.
2.  Download the pending invoice.
3.  Transfer the commission amount.
4.  Upload the proof of transfer in the interface to unlock your account.

### 2. For Advertisers (Clients)

You wish to broadcast an advertisement on a screen in the Shabaka network.

#### 2.1 Booking a Space
1.  Scan the **QR Code** displayed on the screen or click on the booking link provided by the venue.
2.  You access the screen's page with its rates and availability.

#### 2.2 Choosing Your Booking Mode
The system offers two options depending on your needs:

**Option A: Purchase of Plays (Classic)**
*   You buy a stock of plays (e.g., 100 plays).
*   Your ad is broadcast until the stock is exhausted.
*   *Ideal for: A flash promotion.*

**Option B: Period Campaign (Date to Date)**
*   You choose a start date and an end date.
*   You define a number of plays **per day** (e.g., 20/day for 7 days).
*   The system calculates the global price and guarantees fair distribution.
*   *Ideal for: Brand awareness.*

#### 2.3 Ad Configuration
*   **Media Type**: Still Image or Video.
*   **Duration**: Select the display time (e.g., 15 seconds).
*   **Period**: Choose the time of day (Morning, Noon, Evening).

#### 2.4 Uploading Content
Strictly respect the technical constraints:
*   **Resolution**: Must be identical to that of the screen (e.g., 1920x1080).
*   **Size**: Max 100 MB.
*   **Format**: JPG/PNG for images, MP4 for videos.

### 3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: My screen remains black, what should I do?**
A: Check the player's internet connection. If it has been offline for more than 2 minutes, it appears as "Offline" in the dashboard. Restart the player's browser.

**Q: Can I broadcast live TV?**
A: Yes, if the option is enabled on the screen ("IPTV"). Provide a stream URL (M3U8).
